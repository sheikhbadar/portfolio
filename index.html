About Me

Sheikh Badar Ud Din Tahir is dedicated researcher specializing in Statistics, healthcare, Sports Analytics, and Machine Learning . Sheikh leverages data-driven techniques to extract insights in sports, contributing to cutting-edge solutions and advanced methodologies in the field.

An accomplished researcher, he focuses on AI and machine learning, with expertise in predictive modeling, statistical modelling, deep learning, IoT, sensors, and signal processing. Sheikh's impactful research, published in leading journals, has attained over 530 citations , reflecting his significant academic and practical contributions.

Research Focus

Artificial Intelligence

Leveraging AI and machine learning and data analysis to uncover insights in image, videos, signal, and sports data.

Healthcare

Investigating machine learning applications in healthcare data analytics, with a focus on predictive modelling and patient outcomes.

Statistical Modelling

Utilizing advanced statistical methods for data analysis and interpretation to inform decision-making processes in various sectors.
